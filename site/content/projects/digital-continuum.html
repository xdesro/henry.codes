The Digital Continuum

The Digital Continuum site was a project I worked on at Universal Mind for a new framework they were launching. The Digital Continuum as a whole is a huge collection of content which laid out the foundations for enterprise companies to be successful in their digital delivery. I was an interaction developer for this project and helped build out the site, a large-scale static site generator.

Tooling

Digital Continuum is built on Hexo static site generator, uses Contentful, an API-first headless CMS, and deploys with Netlify. The codebase of the site is EJS templates and Sass.

The Project

My primary responsiblilities for this project were templating and styling. A senior developer created the content models for the site’s content and provided API endpoints for templates to hook into.

The content modeling is fairly complex and is broken down into Mindsets, Segments, and Elements — Elements are children of Segments and Mindsets. One element can relate to multiple mindsets, but can only occur in one Segment, etc.
