litsmart e-discovery

The Litsmart E-Discovery site was a Drupal project I worked on while working at Alloy Magnetic that required some scrolling animations. Most of the Drupal site was built by another team member, and the scrolling animations were my main contribution to this project.

Tooling

The base of the site was built on Drupal, but the scrolling animations use HTML5 canvas, jQuery, and ScrollMagic. Styled with Sass, of course.
